Krabby Patty

Description

Embark on an underwater culinary adventure with our Krabby Patty recipe, inspired by the beloved animated series, SpongeBob SquarePants. Join SpongeBob and his friends in the culinary delight of crafting the legendary Krabby Patty, a burger cherished by Bikini Bottom residents and food enthusiasts alike. Whether you're a Krusty Krab regular or a first-time flipper, this recipe will transport you to the heart of the ocean for a taste of whimsical nostalgia.

Ingredients

For the Krabby Patty Patties:

  • 1 pound ground beef (80% lean)
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped pickles
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 hamburger buns

For the Secret Krabby Patty Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon yellow mustard
  • 1 tablespoon sweet pickle relish
  • 1/2 teaspoon white wine vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika

For Garnish:

  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomato
  • Sliced cheese
  • Sliced onion
  • Sliced pickles

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Secret Krabby Patty Sauce:
    • In a small bowl, combine mayonnaise, ketchup, yellow mustard, sweet pickle relish, white wine vinegar, sugar, and paprika. Stir until well blended. Refrigerate until ready to use.

  2. Mix and Form the Patty Patties:
    • In a mixing bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, finely chopped onion, finely chopped pickles, egg, mustard, salt, and pepper.
    • Form the mixture into four evenly sized patties.

  3. Cook the Patty Patties:
    • Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
    • Cook the patties for approximately 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
    • If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking and cover to melt.

  4. Assemble the Krabby Patties:
    • Toast the hamburger buns until golden brown.
    • Spread a generous amount of the Secret Krabby Patty Sauce on both the top and bottom buns.
    • Place a cooked patty on the bottom bun.
    • Add lettuce leaves, sliced tomato, sliced onion, and pickles.
    • Top with the other half of the bun.

  5. Serve Bikini Bottom Style:
    • Serve your homemade Krabby Patties with a side of your favorite sea-inspired snacks, and don't forget the sea shanties!
