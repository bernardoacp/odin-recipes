Ratatouille

Description

Indulge in the magic of Pixar's "Ratatouille" with our delectable Ratatouille recipe, inspired by the heartwarming film that brought a rat and a kitchen together. This savory French dish is not only a feast for the senses but also a tribute to the art of fine dining. Just like Remy, the talented rat chef, you too can create a culinary masterpiece that will enchant your taste buds and transport you to the bustling streets of Paris.

Ingredients

For the Ratatouille:

  • 2 large zucchinis
  • 2 yellow squash
  • 2 eggplants
  • 4 ripe tomatoes
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 yellow bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the tomato sauce:

  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the herb oil:

  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley leaves
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Tomato Sauce:
    • In a saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
    • Sauté minced garlic and diced onion until translucent.
    • Add crushed tomatoes and bay leaf. Simmer for 20 minutes.
    • Season with salt and pepper. Remove the bay leaf and set the sauce aside.

  2. Slice and Season Vegetables:
    • Slice the zucchinis, yellow squash, eggplants, and tomatoes into thin rounds.
    • Dice the red and yellow bell peppers, onion, and garlic.
    • Season the sliced vegetables with olive oil, fresh thyme, fresh rosemary, salt, and pepper.

  3. Assemble the Ratatouille:
    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of tomato sauce.
    • Arrange the seasoned vegetable slices vertically in the dish, alternating between zucchinis, yellow squash, eggplants, and tomatoes.
    • Sprinkle the diced onion and bell peppers over the top.
    • Drizzle with a bit more olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
    • Cover the dish with parchment paper and aluminum foil.

  4. Bake to Perfection:
    • Bake for 45 minutes, then uncover the dish and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes until the vegetables are tender and lightly browned.

  5. Prepare the Herb Oil:
    • In a blender, combine fresh basil, fresh parsley, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth.

  6. Serve in Style:
    • Drizzle the herb oil over the Ratatouille just before serving to add a burst of flavor and a touch of elegance.
